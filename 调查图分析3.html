survery results

We have conducted relevant business demand survey on customers, and the main results are as follows.

According to the survey of the question "Which system do you want to use" in the questionnaire, DSS is the most popular choice, indicating that customers may need to deal with the decision-making system more.

According to the survey on whether they can accept that the system will optimize the workflow to a certain extent, we can find that only a few of them have the option of keeping the same.

According to the investigation of the operating system and IT equipment that customers like to use and the expected system interface, it is found that most customers prefer simple and familiar operation, and hope that the new system can be more friendly.
